Fruity Mango Smoothie Bowl Recipe

Fond of mangoes? Then this super delicious smoothie bowl is a perfect treat for your palate.
This simple yet exotic Mango oats smoothie bowl can be made in just a few minutes with some fresh mangoes,
curd, oats, honey, chia seeds to name a few.
A perfect treat for busy weekdays or fun weekends,
this super sweet, tarty and fruity bowl can also be relished with some ice cream or whipped cream.
Drizzle some caramel sauce,
chocolate sauce or honey to add some more sweetness to this delicacy.
